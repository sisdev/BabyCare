Baby Care App

Baby vaccination is one of the most import part of Baby Care. This app assists baby vaccination as follows:
  • Prepare Vaccination Chart
  • Track the vaccination status
  • Email the vaccination Chart
  • Learn more about the vaccination
Reference : Indian Academy Of Pediatrics
Another part of baby care app provides baby feeding information.

*Disclaimer : This app should be used in consultation with your doctor.This application gives you the generic Information.
